Le Pizze

Our authentic pizza is crafted in the tradition of Naples, Italy, the birthplace of pizza. We adhere to the standards set forth by the Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana, using fresh ingredients. We use fresh imported Italian bufala mozzarella, our sauce is created with Italian San Marzano tomatoes, and our freshly prepared hand-pressed dough creates the authentic pliant crust that made Neapolitan pizza famous.
